About Shri Muttuswami Dikshitar

Shri Muttuswami Dikshitar (1775–1835) was one of the Trinity of Carnatic music composers. He contributed significantly to the development of the classical Carnatic tradition with over 400 compositions. His works reflect deep devotion to Lord Shiva and various deities, often written in Sanskrit. Dikshitar is renowned for his elaborate use of ragas and complex compositions.
